To-Do App Help

How Do I Open A List That I've Created? You Can Open A List That You've Created By Pressing The Dropdown Button In The Top Right Cornner.

How Do I Change The Name Of My List? You Can Change The Name Of Your List By Pressing The Then Changing The Text Of The List Name. This Also Works For Renaming Tasks.

How Do I Reorder The Tasks? You Can Reorder The Tasks By Dragging A Task Ontop Of Another To Swap The Two.

Does My To-Do Lists Save? Yes The Lists Are Saved On The Broswer You Are Using.
